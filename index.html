"RECIPE OF CHICKEN CURRY"

Ingredients:

chicken
cooking oil
ginger
garlic
chilli
onion
tomato
salt
coriander

Procedure:

git add
cooking oil
pour cooking oil in the sreel bowl according to the quantity of chicken
onion
when oil gets hot enough then put chopped peices of onion
until onion changes its color move it with the help of spatula inside the vessel
then pour the paste of tomatoes into it
heat it for 5 to 10 mins
chicken
put the peaces of chicken into it
